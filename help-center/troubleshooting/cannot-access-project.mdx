---
title: Cannot Access Project
description: Troubleshoot when a project does not open in Doulot Doulottt.
---

## Symptoms

- Clicking a project name shows an error
- Project not loading or unavailable

## Possible Causes

- Permissions not granted
- Project was deleted or archived

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Ask your team admin to verify your access to the project from the project settings page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh your session">
    Log out and sign back in at `https://doulottt.com` to refresh your permissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
