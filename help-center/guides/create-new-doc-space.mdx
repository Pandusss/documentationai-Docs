---
title: Creating a New Doc Space
description: Step-by-step: Create and organize a doc space in Doulot Doulottt.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    From your dashboard, click on the desired project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Doc Space'">
    Locate and click the "Add Doc Space" button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name and Save">
    Enter a name for your new doc space and click "Save". 
    It will now appear in your project sidebar for adding documentation pages.
  </Step>
</Steps>
